Our Curriculum

Holistic Learning Approach

At Excel Academy, we believe in a well-rounded education that nurtures the intellectual, emotional, social, and physical development of each student. Our curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire students to reach their full potential.

Core Subjects

  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Language Arts
  • Social Studies
  • World Languages

Enrichment Programs

  • Visual and Performing Arts
  • Physical Education
  • Technology and Computer Science
  • Life Skills
  • Community Service

Teaching Methodology

  • Student-centered learning
  • Project-based learning
  • Differentiated instruction
  • Technology integration

Assessment & Evaluation

  • Continuous and comprehensive evaluation
  • Formative and summative assessments
  • Portfolio-based assessment
  • Regular parent-teacher communication
